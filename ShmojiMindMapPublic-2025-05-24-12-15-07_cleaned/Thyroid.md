  * Where is the thyroid?
    * Butterfly-shaped gland located at the base of the neck, just below the Adam's apple
  * Symptoms
    * Difficulty handling cold
    * Changes in vision
    * Fatigue
    * Poop issues

  * Hypothyroidism
    * Symptoms
      * Skin can get dry like the desert
  * Hyperthyroidism
    * Symptoms
      * Skin can become moist and oily
